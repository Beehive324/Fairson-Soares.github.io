What I Learned from Doing 100+ LeetCode Problems

Date: July 11, 2024

Practice is Key

Throughout my journey of solving over 100 LeetCode problems, one of the most valuable lessons I've learned is the importance of consistent practice. Initially, even the easy problems seemed challenging, but as I built a solid foundation, my confidence grew, enabling me to tackle more complex problems efficiently.

Mastering and Knowing Your Data Structures

A solid grasp of data structures is fundamental for solving algorithmic problems effectively. During my practice, I focused on mastering essential data structures such as arrays, linked lists, stacks, queues, trees, and graphs. Understanding when and how to apply each structure optimally became crucial in developing efficient solutions.

Mastering and Knowing Your Algorithms

Algorithms are pivotal in problem-solving. Understanding which algorithm to apply for a given problem is key to achieving an efficient solution.

Conclusion

In conclusion, embarking on the challenge of solving 100+ LeetCode problems has been a tough yet incredibly rewarding experience. It has equipped me with confidence and significantly enhanced my problem-solving skills compared to where I started.

Next Steps

I am committed to continuing this journey. My goal is to tackle challenges beyond 100 problems—reaching milestones of 200, 300, 400, and eventually 500 LeetCode problems.
